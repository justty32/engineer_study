# 宗教學中英名詞對照表

本表收錄宗教學研究中核心的學術名詞，依照學科領域分類，方便自學者閱讀原文文獻與學術對照。

---

## 1. 基礎理論 (Basic Theory)
*   **宗教學** - Religious Studies / Religionswissenschaft
*   **神聖 vs. 凡俗** - The Sacred vs. The Profane
*   **全然的他者** - The Wholly Other (Das Ganz Andere)
*   **現象學** - Phenomenology
*   **功能論** - Functionalism
*   **實質論** - Substantivalism
*   **神話** - Myth
*   **象徵** - Symbol
*   **儀式** - Ritual
*   **通過儀式** - Rites of Passage
*   **閾限性** - Liminality
*   **集體歡騰** - Collective Effervescence
*   **原型** - Archetype
*   **世界軸** - Axis Mundi

---

## 2. 基督宗教 (Christianity)
*   **三位一體** - Trinity
*   **救贖** - Salvation
*   **原罪** - Original Sin
*   **聖事/聖禮** - Sacrament
*   **天主教** - Catholicism
*   **東正教** - Orthodoxy
*   **新教** - Protestantism
*   **宗教改革** - Reformation
*   **福音派** - Evangelicalism

---

## 3. 伊斯蘭教 (Islam)
*   **真主至大** - Allahu Akbar
*   **絕對一神論** - Tawhid
*   **五功** - Five Pillars of Islam
*   **古蘭經** - Quran / Koran
*   **聖訓** - Hadith
*   **伊斯蘭教法** - Sharia
*   **烏瑪 (社群)** - Ummah
*   **吉哈德 (奮鬥/聖戰)** - Jihad
*   **蘇非主義** - Sufism

---

## 4. 佛教 (Buddhism)
*   **四聖諦** - Four Noble Truths
*   **苦** - Dukkha (Suffering / Imperfection)
*   **緣起** - Dependent Origination (Pratītyasamutpāda)
*   **性空** - Emptiness (Śūnyatā)
*   **涅槃** - Nirvana
*   **輪迴** - Samsara
*   **業力** - Karma
*   **解脫** - Liberation / Moksha
*   **大乘** - Mahayana
*   **上座部 (小乘)** - Theravada

---

## 5. 東亞與民間信仰 (East Asian & Folk Religion)
*   **民間信仰** - Folk Religion / Popular Religion
*   **官僚體系模型** - Bureaucratic Model
*   **神聖中介** - Shamanism (巫覡宗教)
*   **風水** - Feng Shui
*   **儒家思想** - Confucianism
*   **道教** - Taoism
*   **靈驗** - Efficacy (Ling)
*   **互惠性** - Reciprocity

---

## 6. 宗教與社會 (Religion & Society)
*   **世俗化** - Secularization
*   **基本教義派** - Fundamentalism
*   **神權政治** - Theocracy
*   **公民宗教** - Civil Religion
*   **魅力型領袖** - Charismatic Authority
*   **新興宗教** - New Religious Movements (NRM)

---

**【自學小撇步】**：讀英文論文時，如果看到 *Numen* 或 *Numinous*，通常指的就是那種讓人感到敬畏的神聖力量。
